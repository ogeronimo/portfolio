Bio

Accomplished web developer and designer with over 10 years of experience creating visually appealing, user-centered, and functional websites and products. After working as an architect, I was soon seduced by the web and decided to become a skilled web builder with a strong understanding of responsive design, UX/UI principles, and modern web standards. Proven track record of delivering high-quality websites that exceed client expectations and support business goals.

-Omar Ruiz Geronimo
