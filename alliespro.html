AlliesPro

Project Description:

AlliesPro is a gig search and payment web-app that started off as a simple chat to send and receive money. However, has evolved into a payment system that allows freelancers and clients to match, collaborate, and securely make money transactions. Freelancers can apply to gigs around their area and get paid on-time. Furthermore, freelance seekers can post jobs and send money. All of this is brought together in one easy-to-use and beautifully designed website– all of this while making these processes more fun and less awkward.

Objective:

Request and receive payments through our Money Request system.

Send invoices that hold the client's money for a period of time. After any given date or goal has been reached the money will be deposited into your bank account.

Request and receive payments through our Money Request system.

Responsibilities:

Design and Maintain User-Centered Website Interfaces: Develop and refine all aspects of the website's design, ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience.

Created and Implemented Branding: Designed the website’s visual identity, including logo, color palette, typography, and overall aesthetics to align with the brand's vision and target audience.

Developed Responsive and User-Friendly Interfaces: Ensured the website was fully responsive across devices and optimized for an engaging and intuitive user experience.

Integrated front-end Functionalities: Set up gig catalogs, payment gateways, and inventory management systems to enable smooth transactions and operations.

Maintained and Improved the Website Post-Launch: Continuously updated the website with new features, design enhancements, and performance optimizations to keep up with evolving user needs.

Programs Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue, Nuxt.js, Node.js, Firebase and other third-party APIs.
