Do it 4 Jesus

Project Description:

DoItForJesus is a prayer and meditation app centered on fostering spiritual growth through the Rosary and promoting its numerous benefits. The app provides guided sessions for praying the Rosary, including all the mysteries—Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious, and Luminous—while offering reflections to help users meditate deeply on each prayer's meaning.
Designed to make prayer accessible for individuals and families, DoItForJesus emphasizes the Rosary's transformative spiritual, emotional, and mental benefits, such as peace, mindfulness, and a stronger connection to God. The app includes features like customizable reminders to integrate prayer into daily routines, scripture-based meditations, and tools for tracking spiritual progress.
While rooted in Catholic tradition, the app welcomes anyone seeking to experience the Rosary's power as a tool for personal growth and a closer relationship with Jesus. DoItForJesus is a valuable companion for both those new to the Rosary and lifelong devotees, inspiring users to make prayer a meaningful part of their everyday lives.

Objective:

Encourage Daily Prayer: Motivate users to incorporate the Rosary into their daily routines through reminders, guided sessions, and progress tracking

Create a User-Friendly Spiritual Companion: Utilize intuitive design and helpful features like scripture-based meditations, customizable routines, and spiritual challenges to make the app a valuable tool for all users.

Promote Family and Community Prayer: Provide tools and content tailored for families, fostering shared prayer experiences and encouraging community participation.

Programs Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Vue, Nuxt.js, Node.js, Firebase and other third-party APIs.
